KIIT UNIVERSITY HOSTEL FEE STRUCTURE (2025-26)

KIIT University offers separate hostel facilities for boys, known as King's Palace (KP), and for girls, known as Queen's Castle (QC). For the 2025-26 academic year, the hostel fees generally range from Rs. 32,000 to Rs. 70,000 per semester, depending on the room type and amenities.

=== HOSTEL FEE STRUCTURE (PER SEMESTER) ===

The fees are uniform for both boys' and girls' hostels across most departments, though some specific schools like the School of Law may have variations.

Room Type          | Occupancy                    | Fees per Semester
AC Room            | 2-Bedded (Double Sharing)    | Rs. 70,000
AC Room            | 3-Bedded (Triple Sharing)    | Rs. 60,000
Non-AC Room        | 2-Bedded (Double Sharing)    | Rs. 46,000
Non-AC Room        | 3-Bedded (Triple Sharing)    | Rs. 32,000
Law Hostel (Non-AC)| 2-Bedded                     | Rs. 36,000

=== MANDATORY ADDITIONAL CHARGES ===

One-Time Admission Fee: A non-refundable hostel admission fee of Rs. 15,000 is required at the time of joining.

Mess Fees: Mess charges are paid separately and range between Rs. 20,000 and Rs. 25,000 per semester (approx. Rs. 36,000 to Rs. 48,000 per year) depending on the meal plan chosen.

Premium Facilities: An additional Rs. 10,000 per semester may be charged for premium options like attached washrooms in certain blocks.

=== KEY HOSTEL DETAILS ===

Capacity: There are 20 hostels for boys and 11 for girls, accommodating over 15,000 students in total.

First-Year Policy: Staying in the university hostel is compulsory for all first-year students.

Amenities: All hostels include 24-hour reading rooms, high-speed Wi-Fi, indoor games, and attached canteens.

Entry Timings: Strict curfews are maintained, typically 7:00 PM for girls and 8:00 PM for boys for first-year students.

Boys Hostel Name: King's Palace (KP)
Girls Hostel Name: Queen's Castle (QC)

Total Boys Hostels: 43 across the University Campus
Total Girls Hostels: 24 across the University Campus

Hostel facilities include:
- Hygienic Kitchen and Dining Hall
- Round the clock Internet and Intranet Facility
- 24-hour Reading Room Facility
- Facility for all types of indoor and outdoor games
- Flooring with marble tiles
- Good quality Indian and Continental Food
- Attached canteens serving quality food
- Dedicated international hostel for foreign students

For the most accurate and up-to-date breakdown for your specific course, refer to the KIIT official website at www.kiit.ac.in.
